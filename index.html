AVP HGFV

The AVP HGFV (Advanced Vertical Power Harnessing Generator for Vertical Flow) is the core technology behind the Harmony Savonius Wind Turbine. It utilizes a vertical axis design, making it ideal for urban environments and areas with turbulent wind conditions.

Key Features:

  • Vertical axis design for compact installation.
  • High efficiency in low wind speeds.
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable energy generation.

How It Works:

The turbine captures wind energy through its unique Savonius rotor design, converting kinetic energy into electrical power. Its simplicity and durability make it a cost-effective solution for renewable energy.
